Art Painting

2024

The 2024 painting series delves into the theme of the rise of technology, with a particular focus on artificial intelligence and its influence on art. Through a range of artistic styles and techniques, the paintings explore how AI is shaping creative processes and transforming the art world. The series reflects on the intersection of human creativity and technological advancement, presenting a nuanced view of how AI is both a tool and a collaborator in artistic expression. This body of work aims to provoke thought about the evolving role of technology in art and its implications for the future.
