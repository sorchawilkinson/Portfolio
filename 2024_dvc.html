Design Through Visual Communication

2024

For my 2024 DVC project, I am working on a design for a space boot intended for intravehicular use. The project is part of a scholarship initiative and focuses on creating a functional and innovative solution for astronauts. The current display includes ideation and exploration phases, with additional detailed designs and concepts yet to be added. This work emphasizes the importance of practical and cutting-edge design in supporting space missions, reflecting the challenges and opportunities in developing equipment for space travel.
