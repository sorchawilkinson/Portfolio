Art Painting

2023

My 2023 painting project centered around humanity's connection to nature, exploring the intricate relationship between people and their natural environment. The artworks depicted various scenes that highlight both the beauty and fragility of nature, capturing how human activity intersects with and impacts natural landscapes. Through vibrant and expressive techniques, the paintings convey a sense of urgency and appreciation, encouraging viewers to reflect on their own relationship with the environment. This body of work underscores the importance of preserving natural spaces and recognizing the profound bond between humanity and the world around us.
