Art Design

2024

In 2024, my Art Design project focused on the theme of technology's rise, specifically exploring how artificial intelligence influences artistic creation. This project investigates the integration of AI in art and design processes, examining both the potential and challenges of this emerging technology. The design work illustrates various ways in which AI can enhance creative workflows, from generating innovative patterns and forms to optimizing design efficiency. Through this project, I aimed to showcase how AI tools can serve as collaborators in the creative process, pushing the boundaries of traditional design and offering new perspectives on artistic expression. The work reflects a deep engagement with current technological advancements and their impact on the future of art and design.
