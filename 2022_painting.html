Art Painting

2022

In 2022, my painting work explored the theme of older civilizations, reflecting on their legacies and how they continue to influence our modern world. I aimed to capture the essence of these civilizations through detailed and evocative imagery, portraying their advanced technologies, architectural wonders, and rich cultural traditions. The artworks serve as a visual dialogue between past and present, emphasizing how historical advancements and societal structures have shaped contemporary life. By delving into the visual aspects of these ancient societies, my paintings highlight the continuity of human ingenuity and the enduring impact of historical achievements on our current technological and cultural landscapes.
