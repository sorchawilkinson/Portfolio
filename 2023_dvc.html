2023

In 2023, my DVC project involved designing a workstation desk and a ski patrol building. The desk was conceptualized as a versatile and ergonomic workspace that enhances productivity and comfort. The ski patrol building design aimed to provide a functional and efficient space for rescue operations and staff accommodations. While some images on the page are placeholders, with only one actual design included, these visuals offer a glimpse into the design approach and objectives. The focus was on creating practical solutions that cater to the specific needs of their users.
