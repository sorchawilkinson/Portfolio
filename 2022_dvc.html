DVC

2022

My 2022 DVC project focused on designing practical and innovative urban elements, specifically a coffee cart and a bus stop. The coffee cart was envisioned as a functional and aesthetically pleasing mobile unit, aimed at enhancing the street-level experience with its modern design and user-friendly features. The bus stop design sought to improve public transit comfort and functionality, integrating elements that would make waiting more pleasant. Currently, placeholder images are used to represent these designs, as the original work is not accessible at the moment. These placeholders illustrate the intended direction and design principles of the project.
